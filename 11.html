ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Hyerin Park.
I'm currently studying Business administration in Sogang university, Seoul, South Korea.

I was born in Busan where a number of buildings and beautiful beach are naturally combined. This is why I've always loved eating seafood and just laying down sleeping on the beach.

My childhood has influenced on how I am now in many ways. Before I moved to the center of Busan, I was living in a small town until 10years old where it was surrounded by mountain and old buildings. At that time, I rarely spend my days in house and went outside to hang out with friends.
 All I could have done in my town was running all around the village, looking for salamander, sledding in the back of apartment, and sometimes escaping from a friend who was running over with a dead rat, which still scares me out! Haha.

I guess this is where some part of my personality comes from. I consider myself as a
very enthusiastic and energetic person. Thanks to my childhood, I almost never hesitated to do anything. As long as I realize what I want to do or what I should do, I spend my whole time and energy for the thing that I want.

Since my teenage until now, what I've always loved to do is to learn. l have noticed that everytime I learn something new or more deeply in an area, there comes more things that I can learn. Of course, I didn't really enjoy studying while I was in high school. At that time, I studied because I needed to. However, even in those times, I enjoyed spending my time learning what I like, which are still some of my favorite things to learn.

They are language, drawing and winning strategies of games. Whenever I had free time after finishing the day's study plan, I tried to find ways to learn these things by searching internet, watching videos etc. I remember that I was spending whole day trying to figure out winning strategy of a game that my math teacher gave to me, without completing my study plan.
But, what remains in my memory until now is not the days that I had studied for so long hours, but the day when I was too obssessed with finding winning strategy of the game.

These days, (today is 13,April,2020) What I'm so obssessed with is Computer programming. I was trying to apply for some jobs. But,
I found it so hard to express myself only by a paper of CV. That's how I first came up with the idea of making my own website, and now I'm learning HTML by watching youtube videos and reading some books.

When I find something that I want to learn and start learning it, I feel so alive. I believe that
I would never age if I never stop being enthusiastic for learning. I would never stop learning, and stay young. This is the value that I would keep for my whole life.
